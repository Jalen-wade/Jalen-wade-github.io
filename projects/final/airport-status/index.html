Airport Status

On November 8, 2020, the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) screened 973,020 passengers at U.S. airports, compared with 2.35 million passengers screened in the same weekday one year earlier. “The aviation industry runs on a steady supply of passengers,in March we saw a steady decline in the number of passengers we had, it bottomed out in April at less than 5 percent of what we had in the previous year,” said John E.Potter, president of the Metropolitan Washington Airports Authority. Acording to the TSA, the average of passengers on airplanes has steadily remained in the hundred of thousands whereas the previous year had numbers within the 2 millions. Airlines for America found that for the week of November 10, passenger airline departures were down 47 percent from the previous year.
